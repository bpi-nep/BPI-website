B Polytechnic Institute Pvt. Ltd.

सीप सिकौं, आत्मनिर्भर बनौं, भविष्य सुरक्षित गरौं।

B Polytechnic Institute Pvt. Ltd. (BPI) is a professional technical education, vocational training, and workforce development institute established in Dhangadhi-1, Kailali, Nepal. BPI was founded in response to the acute shortage of trained human resources in the Far-West Development Region — particularly in construction, hospitality, agriculture, electronics, and auto-mechanics.

BPI was initially registered as a private firm by the Office of Small and Cottage Industries, Kailali on October 21, 2008 (2065 B.S.), and subsequently incorporated as a Private Limited Company on September 16, 2011. BPI holds PAN/VAT Registration No. 303337100. BPI has now established its main office in Sukedhara, Kathmandu.

Since its establishment, BPI has contributed to skilled human resource generation across Kailali, Kanchanpur, Doti, Achham, Kathmandu, and the broader Sudurpashchim Province — delivering over 100 training events to more than 1,500 trainees in partnership with ILO, Helvetas, Practical Action Nepal, Alliance Nepal, CTEVT Province 7, and several government bodies.

Vision: Centre of Excellence

Creating a Centre of Excellence for technical education, vocational training, and innovative management approaches — equipping individuals with skills fitting the emerging market needs of Nepal.

BPI–Rojgaar

रोजगार क्लिनिक — सीप भएकाहरूको लागि निःशुल्क करियर मार्गदर्शन।
BPI–Rojgaar has its own dedicated branding and identity — details coming soon.

BPI–Rojgaar is the career counselling and employment guidance arm of B Polytechnic Institute. It operates as a structured Rojagar Clinic — a free, dedicated platform connecting trained youth with credible employment pathways across Nepal and abroad.

Whether you are looking to work in the Gulf, explore Europe, find a domestic job, or start your own enterprise — BPI–Rojgaar's counsellors guide you through your real options, protect you from fraud, and stand with you every step of the way.
