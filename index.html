सीप सिकौं, आत्मनिर्भर बनौं, भविष्य सुरक्षित गरौं।

B Polytechnic Institute Pvt. Ltd. (BPI) is a professional technical education, vocational training, and workforce development institute established in Dhangadhi-1, Kailali, Nepal.

BPI was initially registered as a private firm by the Office of Small and Cottage Industries, Kailali on October 21, 2008 (2065 B.S.).

Vision: Centre of Excellence

Creating a Centre of Excellence for technical education, vocational training, and innovative management approaches — equipping individuals with skills fitting the emerging market needs of Nepal.
