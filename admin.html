Popup Notifications

Manage the slide-in notification cards that appear to visitors on the landing page.

Notifications appear as slide-in cards at the bottom-left of the page. Each is shown once per session, dismissable by the user, and timed with an auto-close countdown. Each notification needs a unique ID — changing it resets the dismiss history for that notification.
